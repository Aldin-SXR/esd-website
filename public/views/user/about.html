About the Club

The Embedded System Design Electronics Club is a student club founded in 2014 at the International Burch University in Sarajevo. The Club is an educational and nonprofitable organization which organizes workshops, presentations, lectures, competitions and similar events for both members of the Club and interested individuals from the outside. The main focus areas of the club include the development and design of embedded systems, hardware and electronics.
